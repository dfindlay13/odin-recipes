Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

The peanut butter jelly sandwich is an iconic food containing peanut butter, jelly, bread, and if your into it some honey for sweetness! This iconic dish has served me as a quick pick me up whenever I'm down, or simply when you have nothing left in the cupboard - it always satisfies.

Ingredients

  • Bread - 2 pieces
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly
  • Honey (optional)

Steps

  1. Get two pieces of bread from the cupboard and place them on the plate separately
  2. Take a butter knife and spread an appropriate amount of peanut butter on one slice of bread
  3. Now take the butter knife and spread an appropriate amount of jelly on the other bread
  4. (Optional) drip some honey onto the slice of bread with peanut butter on it
  5. Join the two pieces of bread together appropriately so you don't get your fingers messy!
  6. Enjoy! Or... cut it in half if you're psychotic and then enjoy
