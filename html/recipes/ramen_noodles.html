Ramen Noodles

Description

Ramen noodles are a favorite asian dish that is easy to make and provides the opportunity for a DIY flavor profile depending on what you're in the mood for!

Core Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • 1 - 2 eggs
  • Water

Steps

  1. Fill pot with water
  2. Turn stove burner on high and add pot of water to the burner
  3. Once water is boiling. Add in your noodles
  4. While the water is boiling - grab a pan and cook your eggs to your desired liking
  5. When noodles are cooked and your eggs are done strain your noodles and put them in a bowl
  6. Add desired flavor to noodles - see some options below!
    • Soy sauce
    • Chicken flavored powder
    • Beef flavored powder
    • Garlic
    • Ginger
    • Chili powder
  7. Add eggs to your bowl
  8. Stir and eat!
